# Stakeholder Update Checklist

- Audience and purpose are clear.
- Status is visible in the first lines.
- Progress is tied to outcomes, not just activity.
- Metrics are included when available.
- Risks are direct and not softened into ambiguity.
- Owners and dates are named.
- Asks or decisions needed are explicit.
- The update is concise enough for the audience.
- Sensitive uncertainty is labeled honestly.

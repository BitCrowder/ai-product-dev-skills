# Discovery Interview Templates

## Interview Plan

```markdown
# Discovery Interview Plan: [Feature / Problem]

## Learning Goal
- Decision to inform:
- Assumptions to test:
- Target participants:

## Script
1. Warm-up: Tell me about your role and workflow.
2. Context: Walk me through the last time you [job/problem].
3. Pain: What made that hard, slow, risky, or annoying?
4. Workarounds: What did you try instead?
5. Frequency: How often does this happen?
6. Cost: What happens if you do nothing?
7. Alternatives: What tools or people are involved?
8. Closing: Who else should we talk to?

## Follow-Up Probes
| Situation | Probe |
|---|---|
| Vague answer | Can you describe the last specific time? |
| Opinion | What actually happened? |
| Strong pain | What did it cost you? |
```

# User Feedback Synthesizer

## 中文简介

**用户反馈综合器**：把访谈、问卷、工单、评论、NPS、销售记录或社区反馈聚类成主题、痛点、机会、代表性引用、优先级和产品建议。

## Overview

Use this skill to turn raw feedback into evidence-backed product insight. The output must preserve user voice while separating quotes, themes, interpretation, and recommendations.

Do not count loudness as priority. Consider frequency, severity, segment value, strategic fit, and evidence quality.

## Workflow

1. Identify feedback source, time window, segment, product surface, and decision to inform.
2. Clean and group raw feedback without losing quotes.
3. Cluster into themes: pain, desired outcome, workaround, blocker, delight, confusion, trust issue, pricing issue, or feature request.
4. Score themes by frequency, severity, affected segment, business impact, and confidence.
5. Extract representative quotes and contradictions.
6. Convert themes into opportunities, product hypotheses, support fixes, research questions, or roadmap candidates.
7. State what evidence is missing and what to validate next.

## Output Contract

Always include:

- source and scope
- theme table
- representative quotes
- severity and frequency
- affected segments
- product opportunities
- recommended actions
- caveats and bias risks

Use `references/templates.md` for synthesis tables and `references/checklists.md` before finalizing.
